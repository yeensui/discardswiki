How to Access

1. Join the Spurs Discord Server.

2. Open the **Activities** tab in any voice channel.

3. Search for and launch **Discoys Discards**.

How do new cards work?

Card Rarity & Drops

New cards are dropped during active sessions. Higher rarities like Gold and Diamond have significantly lower drop rates and often require strategic upgrades.

Collection Strategy

Focus on collecting Lilywhites to build your base. Once you have five, you can move toward your first Bronze upgrade.

Upgrade Requirements

Requirements to merge duplicates into higher tiers:

5 Lilywhites to Bronze
8 Bronze to Silver
12 Silver to Gold
15 Gold to Diamond
