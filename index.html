Server Rank & Card Types

Your Server Rank directly influences your card varieties. Depending on your standing, cards will feature different stats, positions, and unique abilities to help you climb the leaderboards.

The Binder Hub

The Binder is your central inventory. Access it to view your collection, check card performance, and navigate to the upgrade screen to merge your duplicates.
