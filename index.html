Ranks & Card Variants

The Diamond Challenge

Packing Diamonds is rare and rewarding. Cards are granted based on your Server Rank, with multiple versions available.

Dynamic Positions

Depending on your standing, you can collect different types of cards. The same player can feature different stats and positions, allowing for diverse tactical builds.

Super Rare Icons

Luck-based Super Rare versions are the hardest to pack. These icons **cannot be upgraded** using base cards.

Card Trading

You can now trade cards with other users! Swap duplicates to complete your collection or build your ultimate house squad.

The Binder Hub

The Binder is your collection's home. It only displays your personal library and is the central place to trade players or upgrade your duplicates.
